Randomized Form

A project by Sean Catangui exploring the concepts of infinite uniqueness, computational art, and composition by chance.

Tapping or clicking each section will generate a completely new, one-of-a-kind composition.

About This Project

✕

Currently, digital is largely utilized as a means to distribute or mimic existing media such as photography, text, video, or graphics. Randomized Form started as an examination of what makes digital unique as an artistic medium. In an attempt to identify that uniqueness, I found inspiration in the art of Ellsworth Kelly, Liam Stevens, and Josef Albers, amongst many others.
